# OSINT of Brazil - UNISHKA Research Service

URL: https://unishka.substack.com/p/osint-of-brazil
Date: Feb 20, 2026
Author: UNISHKA Research Service (Collaborator: Mikel Viteri)

## Summary
A comprehensive OSINT toolkit for Brazil, part of UNISHKA's OSINT World Series. It provides a wide range of public and private sources for investigations related to open data, business, property, people, aviation, legal proceedings, elections, crime, security, human rights, and the Amazon.
