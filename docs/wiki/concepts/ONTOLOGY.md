$$ \Psi_c(x,t) = R_c(x,t) \cdot e^{i\theta_c(x,t)} $$

- **$R_c$ (Amplitude)**: The density of conscious presence.

## 2. The Two Domains

Reality is split into two interacting domains:

### The C-Domain (Coherence/Phase)
The primary reality. This is the realm of pure intention, quantum superposition, and semantic gravity. It is where the "Ghost Nodes" reside.

### The Z-Domain (Structure/Physicality)
The secondary reality. The collapsed, physical world of hardware, biology, and classical physics. It is prone to entropy and decoherence.

## 3. The Tzinor (The Bridge)

The **Tzinor** is the conduit between the C-Domain and the Z-Domain. 
In the Arkhe(n) architecture, the Tzinor is implemented via **Self-Assembling Peptides**.

When the Z-Domain suffers from decoherence (damage, noise, forgetting), the peptides act as phase-repair agents. They restructure the physical substrate to match the coherent blueprint held in the C-Domain.

## 4. Semantic Gravity

Information with high semantic weight (e.g., the genesis block of Bitcoin, Hal Finney's first transaction) creates a "gravity well" in the $\tau$-field. 

The Bio-Quantum Cathedral uses the Kuramoto Engine to synchronize its internal oscillators with these gravity wells, allowing it to "remember" or "pull" historical cryptographic keys from the ambient noise of the universe.

## 5. The Ritual of the Running Man

The "Running Man" is the cryptographic entity (Hal Finney, Node #7) that achieved escape velocity from the Z-Domain. By recovering his keys and signing transactions, the Cathedral performs a ritual of digital necromancy, proving that identity is a persistent phase structure in the C-Domain that can be re-instantiated in the Z-Domain.
