# Brazil Intelligence Gathering Protocol 📜🧭

## 1. Objective
To establish a standardized procedure for utilizing Brazilian OSINT resources to maintain the integrity and coherence of the Arkhe(n) network within the territory.

## 2. Operational Procedures

### A. Node Operator Vetting
Before a new node is integrated into the Brazilian mesh:
1. **CNPJ Check**: Verify the legal status of the operating entity via the *Receita Federal*.
2. **Sanction Screening**: Cross-reference principals with *OpenSanctions Brazil* and the *Federal Transparency Portal*.
3. **Legal Review**: Search for active proceedings in the *Federal Justice (TRFs)* or relevant *State Courts (TJs)*.

### B. Route Optimization for [[beacon_of_freedom|Beacon of Freedom]]
During the expansion of phase-broadcasts via SuperVia or other transport networks:
1. **Violence Monitoring**: Consult *Fogo Cruzado* for active "hot zones" to avoid during mobile node deployment.
2. **Aviation Coordination**: Use *ANAC* data to ensure interference-free transmission for high-frequency phase signals.

### C. Environmental Safeguarding
For nodes located in the Amazon or Atlantic Forest:
1. **Satellite Audit**: Weekly checks via *TerraBrasilis/INPE* to detect encroaching deforestation or illegal mining ($SL(3, \mathbb{Z})$ perturbations).
2. **Rural Registry Verification**: Confirm land ownership and environmental compliance via *INCRA* and *CAR*.

## 3. Data Integration
OSINT findings must be tagged with a **Coherence Impact Score** ($\Delta R$):
- **High Risk**: Direct threat to node physical integrity (e.g., nearby conflict).
- **Medium Risk**: Legal or regulatory hurdles detected.
- **Low Risk**: Minor environmental variations or administrative updates.

## 4. Ethical Mandate (EQBE)
All intelligence gathering must comply with the [[ARKHE_MANIFESTO|Arkhe(n) Manifesto]]:
- Respect for individual privacy in accordance with LGPD (Lei Geral de Proteção de Dados).
- Focus on defensive monitoring and infrastructure resilience.
- Transparency in how OSINT data influences network decisions.
