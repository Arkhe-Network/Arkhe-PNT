# Global Intelligence Gathering Protocol 📜🌍

## 1. Objective
To establish a standardized procedure for utilizing global OSINT resources to maintain the integrity and coherence of the Arkhe(n) network on a global scale.

## 2. Operational Procedures

### A. International Node & Partner Vetting
Before integrating any international node, relay, or partner into the Cathedral's mesh:
1. **Identity Resolution**: Use *Epieos* or *SynapsInt* to verify the digital footprint and history of key operators.
2. **Username Correlation**: Cross-reference handles across global platforms using *WhatsMyName* or *Sherlock* to detect potential adversarial histories.
3. **Reputation Assessment**: Consult *GreyNoise* or *Pulsedive* for domain/IP reputations to ensure no history of malicious activity.

### B. Infrastructure Resilience & Asset Shielding
For global deployments and mobile nodes:
1. **Maritime & Aviation Monitoring**: Utilize *ADS-B Exchange* and *VesselFinder* to coordinate relay node deployment and monitor environmental interference.
2. **Surface Mapping**: Scan global infrastructure using *Shodan* or *Censys* to identify exposed assets that could become points of decoherence.
3. **Conflict Awareness**: Monitor *Liveuamap* or real-time cyber threat maps for regional instabilities that could affect node physical integrity.

### C. Cognitive Shielding & AI Integrity
To protect the Cathedral's cognitive layers (C-Domain/LLMs):
1. **Vulnerability Probing**: Regularly scan any model or interface with *Garak* to detect potential prompt injections or logic failures.
2. **Tactical Mitigation**: Utilize *MITRE ATLAS* to identify known adversarial techniques and implement corresponding defensive protocols.
3. **Standards Alignment**: Ensure all AI implementations comply with the latest *OWASP Top 10 for LLM* security guidelines.

### D. Financial Audit & Crypto Compliance
For nodes or partners involved in the τ-field economy:
1. **Scam Detection**: Check all addresses against *Chainabuse* and other global scam databases.
2. **Entity Analysis**: Use *Arkham Intelligence* to verify the institutional status and historical behavior of crypto entities.

## 3. Data Integration & Analysis
All global OSINT findings must be tagged with a **Coherence Impact Score** ($\Delta R$):
- **High Risk**: Direct threat to the bio-quantum interface or physical node safety (e.g., active conflict, AI vulnerability detected).
- **Medium Risk**: Infrastructure exposures or questionable partner histories.
- **Low Risk**: Minor logistical variations or administrative updates.

## 4. Ethical Mandate (EQBE)
All global intelligence gathering must comply with the [[ARKHE_MANIFESTO|Arkhe(n) Manifesto]]:
- Respect for individual privacy in accordance with international data protection laws (e.g., GDPR).
- Focus on defensive monitoring and infrastructure resilience.
- Transparency in how OSINT data influences network decisions.
